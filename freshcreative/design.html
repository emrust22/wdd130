Feeling fresh – funky designs with a minimalist touch. A colorful palette but not cluttered.

Presentation Slide Decks – made by a design aficionado trapped in the body of a corporate girlie

My design style has recently shifted – and I am working hard to drill down to the exact elements that will be my trusty pieces. I feel re-energized that the entire design world is revolving more around color, retro elements, hand drawn illustrations and blending all these elements with clean lines and minimalist formats.

I spend most of my time designing looking at or working on fonts. The power of words is great, but the power of pretty words. Even greater. I think that working on designs with this wholistic approach helps continue to inspire my process. From start to finish – I work on finding inspiration. Often, my work greatly shifts from where I have started in brainstorming to where I end up with the deliverables, but I enjoy it so deeply.

I have no preferred medium; I love digital deliverables as much as completing work on textiles. I think that all the different quirks of each medium can play so well into how you ultimately choose to complete a project.
