Welcome to fresh creative. by emily rust

I am Emily and this is a virtual home for all my fun fresh ideas. I love graphic design. I am often found not doing what I should be but rather messing around in adobe to create a new t-shirt design, a new wallpaper, or a new slide deck. For what purpose you might ask? Most of the time, no reason other than I had an idea and I wanted to get it down on paper (virtual paper that is).

I am working hard to shift my passion into my career. Currently, I am grinding on that student life – up all night, too much caffeine and all the workarounds to certain copyrights. I have started my second bachelor’s program – ultimately to get certifications in software and web development. I hope to be able to combine my passion for creating and my intrigue of tech into a career in web development. You may just be able to find some of those fresh projects housed here too.

Hopefully you find what you are looking for, and if not, hopefully you at least found something pretty to look at.
